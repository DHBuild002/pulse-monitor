# Pulse

A real-time global intelligence dashboard tracking geopolitics, technology, public health, and science.

## What it does

Pulse aggregates live news and social data from sources across the world and presents it in a single interface. A world map shows where stories are happening, a stories panel surfaces the top headlines by topic, and a social feed pulls in real posts from Reddit, Hacker News, and Bluesky.

**News sources** — Al Jazeera, AP News, BBC World, Deutsche Welle, South China Morning Post, TechCrunch, Ars Technica, NDTV, CDC, NASA, Nature, AllAfrica, Merco Press, Moscow Times, Bangkok Post, and ABC Australia.

**Topics** — Geopolitics, Tech & AI, Public Health, Science & Space.

**Social feed** — Live posts from Reddit, Hacker News, and Bluesky. Supports keyword search across all three platforms simultaneously. Auto-refreshes every 30 minutes with a pause control for testing.

**Map** — Interactive world map with colour-coded markers per topic. Click any marker to read a story summary.

**Reader** — Click any story to open a summary panel with a direct link to the original source.

**Data freshness** — News is cached for 24 hours and served from the browser. Social posts are cached for 30 minutes. The Refresh button forces a live fetch at any time. Page reloads never trigger unnecessary requests.
